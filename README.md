## Features
- photoresistor to control when LEDs will be on (dark) and off (light)
- capacitor for a cool light fade effect on the LEDs
- toggle button switch to turn off LEDs even when dark outside

## Bill of Materials
- 1 Battery Holder
- 1 2.2M Resistor
- 1 47k Resistor
- 1 4.7k Resistor
- 1 Potentiometer
- 1 47k Resistor
- 2 470 Resistors
- 2 LEDs
- 1 470nF Capacitor
- 1 10uF Capacitor
- 1 Photoresistor
- 1 100k Resistor
- 1 220 Resistor
- 3 NPN Transistors
- 1 Push Button
